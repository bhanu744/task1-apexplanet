Basics of Cybersecurity

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and damage.

Common Threats

  • Phishing: Tricking you into giving personal information via fake emails or websites.
  • Malware: Malicious software that can harm your device or steal data.
  • Weak Passwords: Easy-to-guess passwords make it easier for hackers to break in.
  • Public Wi-Fi Risks: Open networks can expose your data to snoopers.

Want to Stay Safe?

  • ✅ Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
  • ✅ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) where possible.
  • ✅ Don’t click on suspicious links or attachments.
  • ✅ Keep your software and antivirus updated.
  • ✅ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions.
